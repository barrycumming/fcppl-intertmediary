Accessibility

First Call Group is dedicated to making this Website accessible to everyone that has access to the Internet. However there are circumstances beyond our control that can lead to one user’s browsing experience differing to another’s. Examples of these circumstances are described as, but not limited to, the following:

Cross Browser Compatibility:

When our Website is viewed in one type of web browser (and different versions of that web browser) such as Internet Explorer, a user of our Website may have a different experience than if they were to use another browser such as Google Chrome, Mozila Firefox, Apple Safari, Opera or others. Equally, with the recent evolution of the Smartphone and Tablets, when viewing our Website on such devices, users may have a further different experience.

Disability:

If users of our Website are visually impaired or have a disability that has an impact on web browsing, they may have a different experience of our Website than those who do not. For example, the use of Accessibility Software such as JAWS or Lynx may be required.

Our Accessibility Actions

In order to provide a web browsing experience that is as equal as possible for all users of our site, we have made substantial efforts to ensure that pages within this Website are compliant with The World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) Standards. One of W3C’s primary goals is to make the social value of the Web “available to all people, whatever their hardware, software, network infrastructure, native language, culture, geographical location, or physical or mental ability. “This is why we have chosen to try and make every page we publish W3C compliant."

Use of PDF Files

Our Important Documents show links to several PDF documents. The availability of these documents is imperative to our customers so that they may fully understand the insurance services we offer. PDF stands for Portable Document Format and can be read using Adobe Acrobat Reader, a free piece of software that can be downloaded from the Adobe website**.
